How It Works?

Mint and manage your unique domain name NFTs on X Layer with our Launchpad!

  • Connect Your Wallet: Use MetaMask to connect to X Layer (chain ID: 196). Ensure enough OKB for .okb or ETH for .star/.xdog gas fees.
  • Mint Domain: Select .okb, .star, or .xdog, enter a domain ID (1-15 alphanumeric characters), and mint your NFT.
  • Manual Registration: Copy hex data and contract address for manual registration via wallet with 0 ETH/OKB and hex as msg.data.
  • Bind Domain: In "My Domains", approve your NFT for the DomainNameResolver contract (0x51AEfC36203F0B3612e15c168aDE8ec2401A1507), then bind it to enable domain resolution.
  • Unbind Domain: In "My Resolved Domains", unbind your NFT to retrieve it, clearing the resolution.
  • Resolve Domain: Bind domains in "My Domains" to set their resolved address.
  • Secure & Unique: One domain per address per contract, case-insensitive IDs, low-cost transactions on X Layer.
