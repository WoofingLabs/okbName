How It Works?

Mint and manage your unique domain name NFTs on X Layer with our Launchpad!

  • Connect Your Wallet: Use MetaMask to connect to X Layer (chain ID: 196). Ensure enough OKB for .okb/.ok or ETH for .star/.xdog/.xlayer/.okx gas fees.

  • Manual Registration: Copy hex data and contract address for manual registration via wallet with 0 ETH/OKB and hex as msg.data.
  • Secure & Unique: One domain per address per contract, case-insensitive IDs, low-cost transactions on X Layer.
