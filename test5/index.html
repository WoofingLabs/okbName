How It Works?

Mint your unique BNB domain name NFT on BNB Chain with our Launchpad! It’s simple and fast:

  • Connect Your Wallet: Use MetaMask to connect to BNB Chain (chain ID: 56). Ensure you have enough BNB for gas fees.
  • Mint Your Domain: Enter a domain ID (1-15 alphanumeric characters, e.g., “jace123”). The NFT will be minted with the suffix .bnb.
  • View Your Domains: Fetch your minted domain NFTs, including token IDs and full domain names (e.g., jace123.bnb).
  • Secure & Unique: Each address can mint only one domain, and each ID is unique (case-insensitive). Built on BNB Chain for low fees and secure NFT creation.
